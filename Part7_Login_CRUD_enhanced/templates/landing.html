What's a patient portal and how does it benefit me?

The patient portal is an online site where individuals can access personal health information such as doctor visits and appointments, lab results, medication records, etc. During the Coronavirus Pandemic, this will be a great resource for individuals to keep track of health information and communicate with medical personnel.
